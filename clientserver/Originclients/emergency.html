Emergency Services

Important Contacts

  • Police: 100 (Emergency number)
  • Fire Department: 101 (Emergency number)
  • Medical Services: 112 (Emergency number)

Emergency Procedures

In case of a fire:

Evacuate the building immediately and call the fire department.

In case of a medical emergency:

Call medical services and provide clear information about the situation.
