Emergency Services

Important Contacts

  • Kenya Police Emergency Line: 999
  • Fire Brigade: 999
  • Ambulance Services (St. John Ambulance): 0706 600000
  • Red Cross Emergency Number: 1199
  • Kenya Power Emergency Hotline: 97771

Emergency Procedures

In case of a fire: Evacuate the building immediately and call the fire department at 999.

Medical emergency: Call 0706 600000 for St. John Ambulance services or 999.
